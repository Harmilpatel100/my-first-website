Royal Challengers Bangalore

The Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is one of the most popular and exciting teams in the Indian Premier League (IPL), representing the city of Bangalore. Known for their star-studded lineup, RCB has consistently featured cricketing icons like Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, and Glenn Maxwell. Despite having reached the IPL finals three times (2009, 2011, and 2016), the team has yet to secure the coveted title, often struggling with consistency in crucial moments.
